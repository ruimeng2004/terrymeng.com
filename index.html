About Me

Building medical AI systems with a computational biology lens.

I am a computer science student moving into bioinformatics, with research interests in medical AI, computational biology, multimodal learning, drug interaction prediction, and AI agents for clinical decision support.

My recent work spans CT-based medical image analysis, Alzheimer’s disease clinical data modeling, and graph-based drug-drug interaction prediction. I am especially interested in connecting biological structure signals, machine learning representations, and agentic reasoning systems.
